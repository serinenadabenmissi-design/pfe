Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How quickly will I receive a response?

+

We aim to respond to all inquiries within one business day. For urgent matters, feel free to call us directly during consultation hours.

Can I book a consultation directly from here?

+

Yes! Select "Book a Consultation" in the subject field of the form above, or use the "Book Consultation" button in the navigation. We'll confirm your appointment within 24 hours.

Are consultations available internationally?

+

Absolutely. All consultations are conducted online via video call, making our services available to clients in any country, any time zone.

What languages do you support?

+

Our team currently offers consultations in Arabic, French, and English to best serve our diverse community of patients.

Is my personal health information kept private?

+

Yes. We take privacy very seriously. All personal and health information you share with us is stored securely and never disclosed to third parties without your explicit consent.
